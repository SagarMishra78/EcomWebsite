SamanLelo.com

SamanLelo.com is an e-commerce company headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. The company initially focused on online book sales before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, home essentials, groceries, and lifestyle products. The service competes primarily with Amazon's Indian subsidiary and domestic rival Flipkart. As of March 2017, SamanLelo.com held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry. SamanLelo.com has a dominant position in the apparel segment, bolstered by its acquisition of Myntra, and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones. SamanLelo.com also owns SPay, a mobile payments service based on the Unified Payments Interface.
